Portfolio

Waspit.me

To acommodate a complete redesign of their web platform, Waspit needed a new marketing website to promote all its new features. Working with the creative director, I fully built the marketing website from the ground up using the Yii framework. In addition to the site, I also implemented a backend that was used by the marketing team to manage site content and to keep track of the company's nationwide campus ambassador program.

Swarm Team Portal

The ambassador portal was created for Waspit's remote ambassadors to keep track of their progress during their summer internships. The portal gathered data from the registration system and presented it in a way that kept the ambassadors active and engaged in the program. It was also used by the marketing team to post files, announcements, and to highlight ambassadors who stood out.

Internship Minisite

This minisite was created as a companion to the old Waspit marketing site, mainly as a recruiting tool for its remote ambassador program. The website described some of the new platform's features and hosted a video challenge in which contestants uploaded videos and their friends supported them via social media and voting. Contestants were able to enter the challenge right on the minisite as well.

Spring Break Challenge

To raise awareness for the company's service and ambassador program, Waspit ran a Spring Break Challenge. The goal of the challenge was to send some ambassadors and their friends to Panama City Beach, Florida for spring break. Contestants were able to enter and upload videos right through the challenge site, and their friends had the ability to support contestants through social media and voting.
