Waspit Marketing Site
To acommodate a complete redesign of their web platform, Waspit needed a new marketing website to promote all its new features. Working with the creative director, I fully built the marketing website from the ground up using the Yii framework. In addition to the site, I also implemented a backend that was used by the marketing team to manage site content and to keep track of the company's nationwide campus ambassador program.
